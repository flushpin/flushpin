# Community Guidelines — FlushPin (Draft)

**DRAFT FOR ATTORNEY REVIEW — NOT YET EFFECTIVE**

**Last updated:** [DATE]

FlushPin works because people share helpful, lawful restroom access information. These guidelines explain what we allow and what we remove. They supplement our [Terms of Service](https://www.flushpin.com/terms) and [Safety Notice](https://www.flushpin.com/safety).

---

## Our principles

1. **Help others find lawful access** — not to bypass rules or enter restricted areas
2. **Respect businesses and staff** — their rules always come first
3. **Share only what you know is accurate** — from your own lawful visit
4. **Keep the community safe** — report outdated, harmful, or abusive content

---

## What you MAY share

- Restroom access methods you obtained through **lawful, permitted** access
- Whether a code is required, not required, or staff assistance is needed
- General access type: customers only, ask staff, keypad code, temporarily unavailable
- Accessibility notes (ADA, baby changing) based on your observation
- Corrections when information has changed
- Reports that a code no longer works or a restroom is closed

---

## What you must NOT share

### Access & property
- **Employee-only**, **staff-only**, **maintenance**, or **private** area codes
- Codes obtained by **breaking rules**, **tailgating**, **forcing entry**, or **bypassing locks**
- Codes you were told **not to share** by staff or signage
- Instructions that encourage **trespass**, **theft of services**, or **unauthorized entry**
- Back-door, loading dock, or non-public entrance instructions meant to evade business rules

### Harmful content
- Deliberately **false** or **misleading** information
- **Harassment**, threats, hate speech, or discriminatory content
- **Private personal information** (employee names, home addresses, private phone numbers)
- Content that endangers **minors** or violates child safety laws

### Abuse of the platform
- **Spam**, duplicate submissions, or automated bulk posting
- **Manipulated ratings** or fake confirmations
- **Multiple accounts** to evade bans or moderation
- **Scraping** or reselling FlushPin data without permission

---

## Access code submissions — your responsibility

When you submit a PIN or access code, you confirm that:

- You accessed the restroom through **lawful means**
- You are sharing information **in good faith** to help others with legitimate needs
- You understand the business may **change or revoke** access at any time
- You will **not** hold FlushPin responsible if access is denied

---

## How we moderate

FlushPin may, at any time and without notice:

- Remove, hide, or edit submissions
- Restrict or terminate accounts
- Block submissions for opted-out businesses
- Label content as "Community shared," "Unverified," or "Business provided"
- Preserve logs for safety, legal compliance, and dispute resolution

We are not obligated to pre-screen all content.
