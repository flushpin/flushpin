## 1. Purpose

FlushPin lists **community-submitted** restroom access information for third-party businesses and locations. We respect the rights of business owners and authorized representatives to:

- **Correct** inaccurate information
- **Claim** and manage their listing
- **Opt out** of public access-code sharing
- **Request removal** of their location from FlushPin

This policy describes how we handle those requests. It supplements our [Terms of Service](https://www.flushpin.com/terms).

---

## 2. Who may submit a request

A request may be submitted by:

- The business owner
- An authorized manager, franchisee, or property manager
- Legal counsel representing the business
- A landlord or property owner (for the premises)

We may request **verification** of authority, including:

- Business email domain matching the business
- Phone verification to a listed business number
- Documentation of ownership, franchise agreement, or management authority
- Government business registration (optional)

We may deny requests we cannot verify.

---

## 4. Types of actions we may take

At our discretion, after review:

| Action | Description |
|--------|-------------|
| **Full removal** | Remove the business/location from public listings |
| **Access suppression** | Keep location visible but hide all access codes and PINs |
| **Status update** | Mark as "customers only," "ask staff," "not publicly available," or "closed" |
| **Edit** | Correct name, address, or access type per verified request |
| **Block re-submission** | Flag location to prevent new community PIN submissions (where technically feasible) |
| **No action** | If request is incomplete, fraudulent, or not from authorized party |

---

| Step | Target |
|------|--------|

| Complex or disputed cases | Up to 30 business days |

Emergency safety requests may be prioritized.

---

## 6. What happens after opt-out

Once a verified opt-out or removal is processed:

1. Public access codes and PINs for that location will be **removed or hidden**
2. The location may show as **"Access information not available"** or be **delisted**
3. We may **log and block** repeat community submissions for that location ID or Google Place ID
4. We may retain **internal records** of the request for legal compliance and dispute resolution
5. **Cached or third-party copies** (app installs, screenshots, search engines) are outside our control

---

## 7. Disputes and re-listing

If a business later wishes to **re-enable** community sharing or provide official access information:

- Submit a new request via business@flushpin.com
- We may require verified business account claim
- Business-provided information may be labeled **"Business provided"** vs **"Community shared"**

---

## 8. User-submitted content after opt-out

If a user submits access information for an opted-out location:

- Submission may be **automatically rejected** or queued for review
- User may receive notice that the location does not accept public sharing
- Repeat abuse may result in account restriction under our Terms and Community Guidelines

---

## 9. No admission of liability

Processing a takedown or opt-out request does **not** constitute an admission that FlushPin violated any law or that listed information was unlawful. We act as a **neutral platform** to balance community utility and business rights.
